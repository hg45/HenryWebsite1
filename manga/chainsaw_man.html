Chainsaw Man

by Tatsuki Fujimoto

Chainsaw Man is a comedic dark urban fantasy horror manga about an organization that fights devils. Devils are manifestations of humanity's negative emotions towards any certain topic. In the beginning, Denji, the protagonist, is a homeless poor teenage boy in his late father's debt. He barely gets bye and eats trash with his devil companion, Pochita. To get along, Denji has to hunt devils for the mafia. When this gets them into a near death situation, Pochita, the Chainsaw Devil, fuses with Denji to save his life. After this, Denji finds himself joining the Public Safety Devil Hunters. This is where the story really starts.

I have read 7 of the 11 total volumes, and so far it's been great. I've loved it. It's bloody and it's hilarious. I would very much recommend this series to anyone.
