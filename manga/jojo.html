JoJo's Bizarre Adventure

By Hirohiko Araki

JoJo's Bizarre Adventure is a legendary manga and anime series by Hirohiko Araki.

Where it all began, Part 1: Phantom Blood

Part 1 of JoJo's Bizarre Adventure, Phantom Blood began the several century, multiverse spanning rivalry between Dio and the Joestars. It starts when Dio's father, Dario Brando, saves Jonathan Joestars (JoJo)'s father's life. Dio later gets invited to the Joestar household, and is a terrible person, even killing the dog. Dio finds a stone mask which makes him an immortal vampire with superhuman abilities. With this newfound strength, he burns Jonathan's house down and kills and eats countless people. JoJo goes on a quest to stop him. The rest is history.
If you plan on getting into JoJo's (which you should, it's great, and I've never met someone who's seen most of JoJo's and doesn't love it), DO NOT SKIP PARTS. Start with Part 1, and then go in order.

JoJo's is legendary.

JoJo's Bizarre Adventure now has 8 parts, 6 parts animated, and is one of the highest grossing manga of all time. JoJo's has a very dedicated following, and is a huge cultural icon in Japan. Many creators make references to JoJo's in their works, colloquially known as 'Jojo references'. People who enjoy JoJo's Bizarre Adventure often make these JoJo references themselves. Part 7 of JoJo's Bizarre Adventure is the second best rated manga on the most popular manga review website and the third best rated manga on the second most popular manga review website. JoJo's has collaborated with both Gucci and the Louvre.
